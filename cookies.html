Cookies & Digital Flavor Preferences

Just as we caramelize onions slowly, we handle your digital footprint with patience, clarity, and respect. This Cookies Policy explains the minimal, privacy-first cookies we serve — no third-party tracking, no hidden crumbs.

What are cookies? (a gentle explanation)

Cookies are tiny text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help remember your preferences, understand site usage, and improve your experience. At Yugen Gastronomy Cipher, we treat cookies like humble spices — used sparingly, with purpose, and never overwhelming your privacy.

🍪 Cookies we use — transparent and minimal

Analytics — simple, masked & respectful

If you give consent via the toggle above, we use a privacy-first, cookieless analytics setup (such as aggregated server logs with IP truncation). No Google Analytics or external tracking scripts are loaded unless explicitly opted-in — and even then, data is anonymized. We only measure page views, referrer types, and broad geographic trends to enhance our content for fellow gastronomy lovers.

3rd-Party Services & Embedded Content

Our website does not embed external video players, social media buttons, or advertising widgets. In rare cases (e.g., featuring a public cooking event), we may include a plain link only. This ensures no third-party cookies are dropped without your direct interaction. We never integrate Facebook, Instagram, or Twitter widgets.

Your rights & how to manage cookies

You can also control cookies via browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies entirely, but essential site features may be affected. For instructions, visit your browser’s help section (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). Additionally, you may opt out of any analytics by keeping the analytics toggle off or by sending a request to privacy@yugengastronomy.com.

Our sweet promise: No dark patterns, no “cookie walls,” and we never use cookies to sell data. This policy is aligned with GDPR, ePrivacy, and CCPA transparency requirements.

Frequently asked cookie questions

  • Do you use cookies for marketing or retargeting? Never. We don’t engage in any advertising or retargeting networks.
  • How can I withdraw analytics consent? Use the toggle above or clear your browser cookies. Your "yugen_consent_pref" will be updated.
  • Are cookies safe? Yes, and we only use first-party cookies with no access to your personal files.
  • What if I disable all cookies? You can still browse our recipes, but contact form anti-spam tokens and session cookies help protect form submission. You may disable them, but some features could be less smooth.

For any privacy or cookie-related request, please email privacy@yugengastronomy.com or use the privacy request form on our Privacy Policy page.
